Cornelius

Cornelius was this guy who worked for Pilate. He was bad because he did what Pilate ordered him to, but then he was forgiven by JoAnna and he was like, "OMG!" and cried. Then he went to Peter and Mary and was baptized and was really happy. He believed in Jesus and was somewhere where he had never been before. But then he was serious again when he like, "Wait a minute guys... I'm Roman and I have to follow Pilate's orders still." And they were like, "Poor you, Cornelius!"
