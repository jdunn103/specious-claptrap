Abraham

Abraham was a man who God spoke to. He brought everybody in his town to the Promised Land. He had Ishmael with Hagar, he had Isaac with Sarah (his wife), and he had other not as important children with Keturah. One day God asked him to sacrifice Isaac so Abraham took Isaac up a hill and right when he stabbed him an Angel appeared and made the knife miss Isaac and then the Angel made a goat appear to sacrifice to God instead of Isaac.
