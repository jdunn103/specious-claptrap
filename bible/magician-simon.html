Magician Simon

Magician Simon was a magician by the name of Simon who performed fake magic. He was performing his fake magic in Samaria when Phillip found him there pretending that he could bring a person back from the dead. Phillip was like, "Hey, you! You are not a genuine resurrector! Let me show you audience members how you actually do it because I believe in Jesus so yeah!" And he brought the dead mom back to life. And Magician Simon was like, "Wow, if I had this Jesus person on my side, I could ACTUALLY bring people back to life and then people would actually want to come see my 'magic'." So Magician Simon was baptized by Phillip and then asked Peter for some of his connection with God and Jesus in exchange for a few coins, and Peter was like, "GOD IS NOT WORTH MONEY!!" and God killed Magician Simon.
