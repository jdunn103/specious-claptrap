Simon

Simon was this very interesting guy who was a disciple of Jesus but was mad when Peter accepted Saul as a member of their "group" and so went to the leader of the assassin people and Eva to join them in their killing. He said that he was committed to them, but when he started realizing that perhaps Saul actually was connected to Jesus, he was like, "Oh no... I've put myself in a very sticky situation that's going to be hard to get out of." So it's okay if you kinda don't like him.
